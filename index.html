Our Story

From One Cow
to an Industry

Nandni Dairy Industry is established and run by a group of young and enthusiastic people working in dairy farming and the agri-sector. Nandni was named after the first cow of Nandni Gai Farm "Nandni".

The group started their agri-sector work with this single cow Nandni back in 2012 AD which led them to establish this Nandni Dairy in 2017. This dairy has a large number of products including traditional and modern sweets enriched with the goodness of pure milk produced in Nandni Gai Farm and farmers associated with the dairy of nearby villages.

Currently Nandni Dairy is producing the famous Nandni brand cup Yoghurt, Rawadi, Rasmalai, Rasbhari, and the most recommended sweets by consumers "Peda" including Nandni brand Ghee and famous Kalakand.
